About me

Hi! I am Shivani. I love creating products that make life easier and more enjoyable for people. I have spent over 6 years leading engineering projects across North America, learning how to work with teams, solve complex problems, and deliver results that really matter.

I used to manage multimillion-dollar water infrastructure projects in Canada and the U.S., working with teams from all kinds of backgrounds to balance innovation, compliance, and real-world needs. That experience taught me how to think strategically and make decisions that impact both users and businesses.

Now I am pursuing my MBA at the Rotman School of Management, focusing on product management and strategy. I recently completed a hands-on Product Management course at BrainStation (link to certification), where I built FamLink, a platform that simplifies travel and healthcare for visiting parents of immigrants. That project showed me how exciting it is to turn ideas into real, usable products.

I am passionate about using data, design thinking, and collaboration to create products that people love and businesses value. I am ready to bring my problem-solving skills and user-focused mindset to product management.
